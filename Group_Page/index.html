About Dimensions

Dimensions is an immersive exhibition showcasing the diverse and innovative research projects of our final-year BFA Digital Arts students. Spanning a spectrum of themes—from mythology and memory to AI and abstraction—this collective experience invites you to explore the intersections of tradition and modernity, reality and imagination. Each piece reflects a unique perspective, weaving together narratives of time, culture, and personal connection. Step into Dimensions and discover a world where creativity breaks boundaries, challenges perceptions, and sparks dialogue.
